# n8n-Smart_Resume_Filter
an automation on n8n that filter the cv by viewing requirments

🚀 AnonRecruit: Bias-Free AI Recruitment Pipeline📌 Project OverviewAnonRecruit is an end-to-end automation built on n8n that streamlines the hiring process. It solves the problem of "Resume Fatigue" by using AI to instantly score candidates against dynamic job requirements while stripping away personal identifiers to ensure unbiased hiring.⚠️ The Problem: "The Recruiter's Bottleneck"Traditional hiring is broken by three main issues:Time Consumption: Recruiters spend hours manually reading hundreds of resumes for a single role.Unconscious Bias: Human reviewers often unintentionally favor candidates based on names, locations, or universities rather than pure skill.Keyword Matching Failure: Basic Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) often reject great candidates just because they didn't use the exact "buzzwords" the system was programmed to find.✅ The Solution: "Autonomous Intelligence"This project transforms the hiring workflow into a Zero-Touch Pipeline:Context-Aware Scoring: Instead of keyword matching, the AI understands the nuance of experience and projects.Blind Recruitment: The system creates anonymized profiles, ensuring candidates are judged solely on technical merit.Dynamic Requirements: Employers can update a Google Sheet with new job descriptions, and the AI automatically adapts its scoring criteria.🛠️ Technical Architecture (The n8n Engine)The workflow follows a 5-stage "Nervous System":StageToolFunction1. IntakeGoogle FormCollects candidate data (Name, Email) and CV.2. TriggerGoogle Sheets TriggerFires the workflow only when a new row is detected to prevent duplicates.3. Extractionn8n PDF ParserConverts unstructured PDF binary data into clean, readable text.4. AnalysisOpenAI / Gemini AgentCompares CV text against requirements from a secondary "Job Description" sheet.5. FinalizationGoogle Sheets (Update)"Upserts" the results using Email as a unique key to keep your database clean.🌟 Key FeaturesSemantic Matching: Uses LLMs to find the "best fit" based on project complexity, not just keywords.Gap Analysis: The AI outputs exactly what skills the candidate is missing, helping HR make better interview decisions.Duplicate Protection: Uses "Update or Append" logic so you never have messy, repeating rows in your spreadsheet.Traceability: Automatically generates a direct web-link to the original CV in the final report for easy access.🚀 Future RoadmapDiscord/Slack Alerts: Real-time notifications for high-scoring candidates.Auto-Emailing: Automatically sending "Thank You" or "Next Steps" emails via Gmail node.Voice Screening: Integrating a voice AI to conduct initial 5-minute phone screenings.
